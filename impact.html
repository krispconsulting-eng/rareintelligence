Why it exists

The smallest communities carry the heaviest load.

For ultra-rare conditions there may be no foundation, no awareness campaign, and a handful of families worldwide trying to understand a disease the literature barely mentions. They have the least support and the steepest climb.

The Impact Project channels a share of every paid engagement — and every Advocate membership — into pro-bono intelligence for exactly these communities.

How it works

From request to real help.

1

A community reaches out

A patient group, family network or under-funded org tells us what they're trying to understand.

2

We scope it together

We agree on a question that matters — a landscape brief, a trial map, or a custom search tool.

3

We build it — free

The same SIGNAL Method™ we use for paying clients, delivered at no cost.

4

It stays theirs

Tools, briefs and templates are handed over to keep and reuse, with a check-in down the line.

What we deliver

Real tools, not awareness ribbons.

🗺️

Condition landscape briefs

A clear, sourced map of what's known, what's being studied and where the gaps are.

🔬

Trial & research alerts

Ongoing monitoring so tiny communities never miss a relevant study again.

🤖

Custom AI assistants

A searchable, plain-language helper trained on a community's own condition and documents.

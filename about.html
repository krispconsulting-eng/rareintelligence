About

Built from the gap between information and answers.

Rare disease is drowning in data and starving for clarity. Rare Intelligence exists to close that gap — with a method, a community and a stubborn belief that no one should have to become a researcher to be taken seriously.

Our promise

Sourced. Honest. Human. Yours to question.

The story

It started with a question no one could answer.

Behind Rare Intelligence is a simple, frustrating experience that millions share: a diagnosis (or the long wait for one), a search bar, and an avalanche of papers, forums and trial registries that almost — but never quite — answer the question that actually matters.

So we built the thing we wished existed: a way to read the entire landscape of a rare condition quickly, fairly and in plain language — pairing AI that never gets tired with human judgment that never loses the plot.

Today that work serves patients, caregivers, advocacy organisations and the founders building the next generation of rare-disease solutions.

Mission

Make rare-disease knowledge usable for the people who live it.

Not dumbed down. Not gatekept. Just clear enough to act on — and honest enough to trust.

The method

The SIGNAL Method™

Every brief, build and conversation runs through the same six steps — so the output is always sourced, grounded and yours to act on.

S

Source

Gather the trials, papers, registries and real-world reports that touch your question.

I

Interpret

Translate the evidence into plain language — strength, caveats and what's still unknown.

G

Ground

Anchor every claim to its citation. If we can't show you where it came from, we don't say it.

N

Navigate

Map the options — specialists, trials, communities, questions — into a route you can follow.

A

Act

Turn insight into a concrete next step you can take this week, not someday.

L

Learn

Rare science moves fast. We keep watching, and tell you when something changes.

What we stand for

Principles we won't bend.

Evidence over hype

We'd rather say "we don't know yet" than sell false hope. Every claim is sourced.

People over conditions

You're not a case study. The lived experience is data too — and it leads.

Clarity over cleverness

If a nine-year-old's parent can't use it at 2am, it isn't finished.

Open over gatekept

Knowledge that could help someone shouldn't sit behind a paywall or a PhD.

Honest about AI

AI is a powerful tool, not an oracle. Humans stay in the loop, always.

Never a substitute for care

We make you a sharper partner to your medical team — we never replace it.
